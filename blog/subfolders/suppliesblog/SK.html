In this chapter, I will go through how to clear these stages using only 3 star operators.

The picture below is roughly the team composition I'll be using.

I will just be using 3 star operators, so this should be applicable for everyone.

Because they are all 3 stars, I will call them by their names instead of class abbreviation.

SK-1

Deploy in this order: Fang, Midnight, Adnachiel, Steward, Hibiscus.

This should sufficient enough to finish this stage without any further steps required.

SK-2

Step 1:

Deploy your team in this order:

Vanilla, Fang, Lava, Hibiscus, Steward

Step 2:

Whenever possible, swap out both Vanguards with Cardigan and Beagle. Deploy Ansel to provide additional healing.

Step 3:

A few enemies might leak from Beagle. To stop this, deploy Midnight behind her.

Finale

If you follow this correctly, you should be able to complete this level.
