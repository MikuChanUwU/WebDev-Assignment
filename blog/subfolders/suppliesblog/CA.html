In this chapter, I will go through how to clear these stages using mostly 3 star operators.

I will just be using 3 star operators and a 4 star operator, so this should be applicable for everyone.

Because of that, I will call them by their names instead of class abbreviation.

CA-1

Deploy your team in this order:

Kroos, Adnachiel, Jessica, Hibiscus

This should sufficient enough to finish this stage without any further steps required.

CA-2

Instead of facing your units forward, face them backwards except for Orchid instead.

Deploy your team in this order:

Adnachiel, Jessica, Kroos, Hibiscus, Orchid, Ansel

This should sufficient enough to finish this stage without any further steps required.
