In this chapter, I will go through how to clear these early stages using only 3 star operators.

Before I begin, the team composition I'll be using for all chapter 1 stages will be shown below.

I will just be using 3 star operators, so this should be applicable for everyone.

Because they are all 3 stars, I will call them by their names instead of class abbreviation.

Chapter 1-1

Step 1:

Deploy the operators here as follow:

Fang, Vanilla, Melantha, Hibiscus, Lava

Step 2:

As the Junker approaches, activate Melantha's skill

Finale

If you follow this correctly, you should be able to complete this level.

Chapter 1-3

Step 1:

Deploy the operators here as follow:

Fang, Adnachiel, Vanilla, Kroos, Hibiscus, Ansel

Step 2:

A Junker will start to approach. When said Junker gets into Adnachiel's range, activate his skill.

Finale

If you follow this correctly, you should be able to complete this level.

Chapter 1-4

Step 1:

Deploy the operators here as follow:

Fang, Vanilla, Adnachiel, Kroos, Melantha, Hibiscus

Step 2:

A Junker will come out on the middle lane. Activate Vanilla's and Adnachiel's skills when in range.

Finale

If you follow this correctly, you should be able to complete this level.
