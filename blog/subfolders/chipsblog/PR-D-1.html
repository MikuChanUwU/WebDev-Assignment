In this chapter, I will go through how to clear these stages using only 3 star operators.

Before I begin, the team composition I'll be using for all stages will be shown below.

I will just be using 3 star operators, so this should be applicable for everyone.

Because they are all 3 stars, I will call them by their names instead of class abbreviation.

PR-D-1

Step 1:

Deploy your units as follow:

Fang, Vanilla, Adnachiel, Midnight, Hibiscus, Melantha

Step 2:

Retreat both Vanguards and place down Beagle and Cardigan.

When one of the shielded soldiers approach Melantha, activate her skill.

Retreat Adnachiel and deploy Steward.

Step 3:

When the shielded soldiers approach Cardigan, activate Midnight's skill for him to deal Arts DPS.

Step 4:

There will be leakers from both defenders. To counteract this, redeploy the vanguards to bodyblock the remaining enemies, giving time for the DPS to kill them off one by one.

Finale

If you follow this correctly, you should be able to complete this level.
